About Me

I'm Mark Daryl T. Corpuz, a dedicated and passionate Professional Esports Athlete with nearly four years of competitive experience. Throughout my journey in the esports scene, I’ve competed in some of the toughest tournaments across the country — bringing home championships and constantly pushing my limits. My discipline, teamwork, and in-game decision-making have allowed me to grow with each team I've joined — from local grassroots tournaments to high-level LAN and SM events. I specialize in tactical shooters, particularly VALORANT, where I’ve made a name for myself in role like Duelist. Outside of matches, I’m constantly learning — whether it's reviewing past games, refining strategies, or mentoring teammates. My love for the game goes beyond the competition — it’s about growth, community, and making a mark in the esports industry.
